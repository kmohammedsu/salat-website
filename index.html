Nearby masjids

One prayer. Every masjid. At a glance.

A Weather-style view of the current prayer across every masjid around you — so you can pick the jama’ah you can actually make.

  • Committee-set iqamah front and centre — never an estimate
  • Distance & real driving time to each masjid
  • NEXT highlights the jama’ah coming up soonest
  • Travelling? Mosques near you appear even before we have their times
The whole day

Adhan & Iqamah, side by side

Open any masjid for its full day — every adhan, every iqamah, and Jumu’ah khutbah times — exactly as the masjid publishes them.

  • All five prayers with both adhan and iqamah
  • Jumu’ah takes its rightful place on Fridays
  • Set any masjid as your home masjid in one tap
For every age

A gentle teacher, built in

Flip any prayer card and Salat becomes a quiet lesson — perfect for a child learning to pray, and a beautiful reminder for you, on the way to the masjid.

  • Rak’ah breakdown for every prayer, by madhab
  • A new sunnah every day, with its source
  • Short du’as and the Durood — simply explained

Why Salat

Built as a sadaqah jariyah

The real iqamah times are scattered across flyers, WhatsApp groups, and out-of-date websites. Salat gathers them into one trustworthy place — for you, for your child, for everyone.

“Keeping Salat free of ads, fees, and tracking is my way of contributing to the masjid and the community. In time, I intend to open-source Salat and hand it to the community — so the community itself can keep improving it.

You don’t need to buy me a coffee. If Salat helps you reach the jama’ah, or helps your child learn to pray, please just remember me in your prayers.”

Khaja Moinuddin Mohammed · maker & maintainer

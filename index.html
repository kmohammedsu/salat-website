Features

Everything you need to reach the masjid on time

Most prayer apps calculate. Salat knows — every time you see is the jama’ah time a masjid’s committee actually set.

🕌

Real masjid times

Committee-set adhan & iqamah for every prayer, plus Jumu’ah khutbah times — straight from each masjid, never estimated.

🧭

Compare nearby masjids

A Weather-style view of the current prayer across every masjid around you — with distance and real driving time to each.

✈️

Travelling? Covered.

In a new city, Salat finds the mosques near you — even ones not in our directory yet — so you always know where to pray.

🔔

Reminders, your way

Choose which prayers to be notified for and exactly how many minutes before the jama’ah — sound, vibration, or silent.

🌙

A gentle teacher

Tap any prayer for its rak’ah breakdown, the sunnah of the day, and short du’as — simple enough for children learning to pray.

🎨

Nine calm themes

From deep teal to navy & antique gold — pick a look you (or your kid) love, in light or dark, applied instantly.

Why Salat

Built as a sadaqah jariyah

The real iqamah times are scattered across flyers, WhatsApp groups, and out-of-date websites. Salat gathers them into one trustworthy place — for you, for your child, for everyone.

“Keeping Salat free of ads, fees, and tracking is my way of contributing to the masjid and the community. In time, I intend to open-source Salat and hand it to the community — so the community itself can keep improving it.

You don’t need to buy me a coffee. If Salat helps you reach the jama’ah, or helps your child learn to pray, please just remember me in your prayers.”

Khaja Moinuddin Mohammed · maker & maintainer

Why Salat لِمَاذَا

Built as a sadaqah jariyah صَدَقَة جَارِيَة

Reaching the jama’ah used to take three apps. First, figure out which masjid is actually near you. Then dig through its website for the exact iqamah timings. Then open Maps to work out how to get there. Salat fetches the times straight from each masjid and brings everything to one place: the masjids around you, their exact timings, and the way there. For you, for your child, for everyone.

“Keeping Salat free of ads, fees, and tracking is my way of contributing to the masjid and the community. In time, I intend to open-source Salat and hand it to the community, so the community itself can keep improving it.

You don’t need to buy me a coffee. If Salat helps you reach the jama’ah, or helps your child learn to pray, please just remember me in your prayers.”

Khaja Moinuddin Mohammed · maker & maintainer

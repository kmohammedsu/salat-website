Support

Salat — a community prayer companion

Salat is a free community prayer companion showing the real jama’ah times set by nearby masjids. Support is handled right inside the app — there’s nothing to sign up for.

Get help or send feedback

Open the app and go to Settings → Help & support, then choose:

  • Contact us — questions or anything else.
  • Suggest a masjid — add a masjid to the directory.
  • Report a wrong time — tell us if a jama’ah time looks off.
  • Report a bug — something not working as expected.

You can optionally include your email if you’d like a reply.

Common questions

Where do the times come from? Each masjid’s own committee — not calculated estimates. If a time looks wrong, please use “Report a wrong time”.

Do I need an account? No. Salat has no accounts, no ads, and no tracking, and it’s free for everyone.

Location? Optional — used only to find nearby masjids and prayer times. The app works without it.

My masjid isn’t listed. Tap it in the “More nearby” section and choose Suggest its times, or use Suggest a masjid in Settings — we’ll add it, in shā’ Allāh.
